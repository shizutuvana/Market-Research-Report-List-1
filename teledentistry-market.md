<p><p>Teledentistry encompasses various delivery modes, primarily classified into synchronous and asynchronous types. Synchronous teledentistry involves real-time interactions between patients and dental professionals, typically through video calls, allowing immediate assessment and guidance. In contrast, asynchronous teledentistry involves the exchange of information, such as photos and messages, without real-time interaction, facilitating remote consultations at both ends. Other emerging models may integrate artificial intelligence, remote monitoring tools, and mobile applications to enhance accessibility and streamline dental care, ensuring comprehensive patient support.</p></p>
