Liquid ring pumps use liquid to create a seal, offering efficient gas removal. Gas-circulation-cooled pumps enhance performance by maintaining optimal temperatures during operation. Screw pumps utilize rotating screws for gas compression, ensuring continuous flow and reliability. Multi-stage roots pumps operate in pairs to achieve high vacuum levels, making them suitable for demanding applications. Together, these pumps enable precise control in semiconductor manufacturing environments.</p></p>
