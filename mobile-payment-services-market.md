<p><p>Mobile payment services can be categorized into two main types: remote and proximity payments. Remote payments occur when transactions happen from a distance, typically using mobile apps or online platforms for purchases without physical interaction. This method is prevalent for e-commerce and bill payments. Conversely, proximity payments involve transactions made in close range, often utilizing NFC technology or QR codes at physical stores. These enable quick, tap-to-pay options, enhancing convenience for in-person shopping experiences.</p></p>
