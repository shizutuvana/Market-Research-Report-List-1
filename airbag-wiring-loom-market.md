<p><p>The airbag wiring loom market is categorized into three main types based on their shapes: H-shaped, E-shaped, and T-shaped. H-shaped looms offer a balanced structure, typically used for central airbag systems. E-shaped looms are designed for more complex configurations, allowing for better distribution of wiring across multiple airbags. T-shaped looms, characterized by their linear arrangement, are commonly utilized in vehicles with fewer airbag deployments. Each type is tailored to specific vehicle designs and safety system requirements.</p></p>
