Electric Mast Lifting utilizes an electric motor to raise the mast, providing user-friendly operation and quicker deployment. Hydraulic-pressured Mast Lifting employs hydraulic systems for smooth and rapid height adjustments, offering enhanced stability and versatility for various lighting needs in outdoor and construction settings.</p></p>
