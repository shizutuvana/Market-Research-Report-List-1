<p><p>The Cast Iron Industrial Valve market encompasses several key types: </p><p>1. **Ball Type**: Utilizes a spherical disc to control flow, known for quick operation.</p><p>2. **Butterfly Type**: Features a rotating disc, ideal for throttling and regulating flow in large pipes.</p><p>3. **Gate Type**: Employs a sliding gate mechanism, best for on-off control with minimal pressure drop.</p><p>4. **Check Type**: Prevents backflow using a hinged disc, ensuring fluid flows in one direction.</p><p>5. **Others**: Includes various specialized valve designs catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
