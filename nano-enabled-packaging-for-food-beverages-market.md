<p><p>Nano-enabled packaging for food and beverages enhances product safety and quality through three main types: controlled packaging, active packaging, and intelligent packaging. Controlled packaging employs nanomaterials to regulate factors like moisture and gas, extending shelf life. Active packaging incorporates substances that actively interact with the product or environment to absorb oxygen or release preservatives. Intelligent packaging utilizes sensors and indicators to monitor freshness or temperature, providing real-time information to consumers and helping prevent spoilage, ensuring optimal product quality.</p></p>
